About Us

We at Tesla are responsibly bridging the gap between new technologies and traditional electrical and electronic systems. Our research ranges from revolutionising old school projects like electric power distribution to adapting the newest technologies of AI for load forecasting. We believe in strengthening the rudimentary technologies with the help of emerging technologies.

Tesla Lab for Electrical Systems is focused on research related to core electrical systems mainly focusing on Wireless power transfer, Signal Processing, Embedded systems and semiconductor research, it is expected that you have a basic understanding of electronics and semiconductor theory and a willingness to work hands-on cutting edge research.
